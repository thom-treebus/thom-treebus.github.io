Pacto - The social networking site for university students

Pacto is a social networking website specifically targeted at university students. Students can register using a university email adress and then interact with other students from the same university in sub-communities. People can create posts, leave comments under posts, view each other's profile, create events and more. This project was developed in a team of 8 students over the course of 4 months.

Chess Mate - A chess club management system

Chess Mate is a web app that lets chess clubs manage their club. Users can create an account and apply to join various chess clubs, or start their own! Once approved, they will become a member of a chess club. Officers of a club can create announcements and organize tournaments for members. They can also manage who is part of the club by banning or admitting new members.
